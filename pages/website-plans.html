In-browser Desktop & Tools

    Our apps will be available to use online, so you can access them from any web browser.
    Users can also make profiles, & upload their own files. It's kind of like Google Drive or Dropbox, in that way.




Pages

    Users can also make public pages. Pages can be made from page templates, or hardcoded using the CookBookJS framework.
    Pages & page templates can also be made with our visual page-making app!
