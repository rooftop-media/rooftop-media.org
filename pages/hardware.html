    Below are some guidebooks I've been working on. Unlike the software guidebooks, which provide actionable tutorial steps, these books are purely research based.

3d Printing

The most popular type of 3d printing is FDM printing, where a coil of filament is heated & extruded onto the printer's bed.
This guide goes over FDM printing options -- printers, filaments, etc.
There exist many other methods of 3d printing, aka "additive manufacturing."
I'm also working on a research guide for SLA printing, or "StereoLithographic printing". In this form of printing, lasers are used to harden liquid resin.
